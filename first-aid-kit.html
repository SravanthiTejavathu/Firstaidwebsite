Essential Items for Your First Aid Kit

  • Adhesive Bandages
  • Gauze Pads
  • Antiseptic Wipes
  • Medical Tape
  • Scissors
  • Instant Cold Packs
  • Pain Relievers (e.g., ibuprofen)
  • Thermometer
  • Burn Cream
  • Elastic Bandage (e.g., for sprains)
  • Disposable Gloves
  • First Aid Manual

Make sure to check your first aid kit regularly to replace any used items.
